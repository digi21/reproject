Reproject — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

This privacy policy describes how Reproject (the “app”), published by Dreaming With Objects S.L. (“we”, “us”), handles your information. Reproject is a desktop application for transforming coordinates between coordinate reference systems.

In short Reproject does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us. We do not use accounts, telemetry, analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. Everything the app stores stays on your device.

1. Information we collect

None. The app has no user accounts and does not gather, log, or send us any personal data or usage statistics. We operate no server that receives information about you.

2. Data stored on your device

Your settings, any coordinate reference or geoid grid files you download, and a cached copy of the grid-source catalog (see below) are stored locally on your computer, in the app’s local application data folder. This information never leaves your device and is not shared with us or anyone else.

3. Network connections

Reproject connects to the internet only for the following, strictly functional purposes:

  • Grid-source catalog. On startup, the app downloads a small public JSON file that lists where official coordinate/geoid grids can be obtained. It is hosted on GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com). The result is cached locally so the app works offline afterwards.
  • Grid downloads (only when you ask). When you choose to download a specific grid file, the app fetches it directly from the official third-party agency that provides it (for example, the U.S. National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency or Spain’s Instituto Geográfico Nacional).

As with any internet request, these connections make your device’s IP address visible to the servers you contact (GitHub and, if you download a grid, the relevant agency). Those parties process that information under their own privacy policies — for example, GitHub’s privacy statement. We do not receive, collect, or store your IP address or any other data from these requests.

4. Third-party services

Reproject relies on GitHub to host the grid-source catalog, and on the official geodetic agencies whose grids you may choose to download. We are not affiliated with those agencies, and their websites and downloads are governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Children’s privacy

Reproject is a professional tool and is not directed at children. In any case, it collects no personal information from anyone.

6. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above and publish the new version at this same address.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/digi21/reproject/issues.
